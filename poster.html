Minimalist poster design. Effective use of typography, illustration to convey the theme/message of the film/quote.

The Birds

Poster design for the movie, The Birds. As part of a college assignment, the Alfred Hitchcock movie was chosen for it's dark themed plot. A minimalistic poster to effectively convey the theme with bold typography and symbolism was used to create the poster.

Quote poster

Poster design for the quote from Macbeth. The quote By the pricking of my thumbs, something wicked this way comes, is a famous quote from William Shakespeare's story. The poster was designed bearing in mind the dark theme of the story, with infleunces from De Stijl and Bauhaus movements. The reason for use of elements from De Stijl and Bauhaus movements was to break down the quote to its basic structure and convey the message through rigid forms, to enforce the crudeness of the scenery.
