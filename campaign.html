Campaign Advertisement - Best Buy

A campaign advertisement designed for Best Buy as part of an inclass project. The theme for the campaign was Replacement of old appliances. Wit the iconic Best Buy blue, a simple advertising campaign that targets it consumer base. The advertisement embraces the age of the appliances, to make it interesting to its audience(Families, Men and women over 30) who refuse to acknowledge their age. Bold typography and simple photography carries the message in a fun yet essential way.
