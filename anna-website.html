Website Design - Anna Bélanger & Associates

Website re-design for Anna Bélanger & Associates. The website uses Shopify's theme and customised to suit the needs of Anna's massage clinics. The site aims to make the users feel welcome & comfortable to use the services offered by Anna & her associates. Fertile colours, elegant typography with a classy yet approachable feel has been incorporated throughout the site. Calm, relaxed and warm were the themes chosen for re-design of this website, in line with the positive atmosphere at the clinic.
